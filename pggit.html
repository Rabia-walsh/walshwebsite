Program Overview

Take charge of your career and position yourself for success with the Walsh College Master of Science in Information Technology (MSIT) program. Designed for both current IT professionals looking to advance and those aiming to shift their career path to IT, the Walsh MSIT equips you with the tools and guidance to become an IT expert. With concentrations in Cybersecurity and Data Science, you'll be prepared to excel in two of the fastest-growing IT specializations.

The MSIT program offers a comprehensive curriculum that empowers you to master the multifaceted realm of IT. You will develop versatile coding expertise across diverse programming languages and secure digital assets through comprehensive cybersecurity strategies. The program also focuses on managing and optimizing IT infrastructure, innovating with emerging technologies, analyzing and synthesizing complex data to drive strategic decisions, navigating cloud computing, and troubleshooting IT challenges with technical acumen. As the tech landscape evolves, your diverse skill set will make you an essential player in shaping the digital future.

Program Outcomes

Walsh College’s Master of Science in Information Technology (MSIT) program offers specialized expertise in Cybersecurity and Data Science, equipping you with advanced knowledge in critical IT areas. The program emphasizes hands-on training through interactive coursework and labs, simulating real-world scenarios to enhance your problem-solving skills and technical proficiency. You will develop industry-relevant skills in cutting-edge technologies such as machine learning, cloud computing, and IT infrastructure management, ensuring you are prepared to tackle current and future IT challenges. Designed to enhance career readiness, the MSIT curriculum prepares you for various high-demand IT roles, from cybersecurity analysts to data scientists, providing a strong foundation for both entry-level positions and career advancement.

Additionally, Walsh College’s extensive industry connections offer valuable networking opportunities through internships, workshops, and guest lectures, crucial for building a professional network and discovering career opportunities. The program also focuses on developing critical thinking and problem-solving skills, enabling you to implement effective IT solutions to complex challenges. With a strong emphasis on leadership development, you will gain the skills necessary for guiding teams and projects.

THE MASTERS OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY PREPARES YOU FOR THE CAREERS IN:

  • VP of Business Operations
  • CISO/CTO
  • Cybersecurity Engineer
  • IT Support Analyst

Entry criteria

  • Professional qualifications equivalent to the required academic standards will be considered on a case-by-case basis.
