Program Overview

The future of business is driven by artificial intelligence, and the Walsh College Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning program is your gateway to seizing these opportunities. Whether you're an experienced professional or new to the field, this program equips you with the skills to thrive in an AI-driven world. You'll gain expertise in analyzing and interpreting complex data, mastering machine learning techniques, and applying deep learning principles to solve intricate problems. The curriculum covers essential areas such as natural language processing, computer vision, SQL, database management, and network and operating systems fundamentals. Through hands-on projects and real-world applications, you'll develop programming proficiency and create compelling data visualizations and predictive models.
Walsh College's MS in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning ensures you are well-prepared to meet the demands of modern business environments. The program emphasizes practical experience through interactive coursework and labs, enhancing your problem-solving abilities and technical proficiency. You'll benefit from networking opportunities with industry professionals through internships, workshops, and guest lectures, crucial for building a professional network and discovering career opportunities. The comprehensive curriculum prepares you for various roles in AI and machine learning, providing a solid foundation for career advancement and specialization in this rapidly evolving field. Join Walsh College and become a leader in harnessing the power of AI to drive innovation and success.

Program Outcomes

Walsh College’s Master of Science in Data Analytics equips you with specialized expertise in critical areas like Machine Learning, Data Visualization, and Predictive Modeling. The program emphasizes hands-on training through interactive coursework and real-world projects, ensuring you develop the analytical abilities and technical proficiency needed to tackle complex data challenges. You will gain industry-relevant skills that prepare you to address current and emerging data analytics needs, making you well-prepared for various roles in the field and offering a strong foundation for career advancement. Networking opportunities with industry professionals, through internships, workshops, and guest lectures, are integral to the program, helping you build a robust professional network. The curriculum fosters critical thinking and problem-solving skills, essential for implementing effective data-driven solutions. Leadership development is also a focus, with courses on management and strategic decision-making in data analytics, preparing you to guide data teams and projects. With a global perspective and adaptability to new technologies, the program ensures you stay relevant in the fast-paced data analytics landscape, providing a solid foundation for further education and enhancing your careerprospects.

Entry criteria

  • Applicants should typically hold an undergraduate degree with at least a 2:2 or an equivalent international qualification.
  • Professional qualifications equivalent to the required academic standards will be considered on a case-by-case basis.
  • Work experience is not required, but candidates without formal qualifications who have significant relevant professional experience will be considered individually.
