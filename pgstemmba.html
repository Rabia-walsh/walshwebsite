Program Overview

The STEM Master of Business Administration (MBA STEM) is an advanced program specifically designed for professionals with a solid foundation in business who seek to differentiate themselves by mastering the integration of technology and data within their organizations. This degree is perfect for individuals looking to enhance their technical skills and apply them strategically in a business environment. Unlike traditional business programs, the MBA STEM offers a curriculum that merges technology management with essential business areas like finance, security, project management, and leadership. This comprehensive approach not only deepens students' understanding of the rapidly evolving technological landscape but also equips them with the skills to lead innovative and forward-thinking organizations. Whether you aim to progress in your current position or shift to a new career, the STEM MBA provides the knowledge and tools necessary to thrive in today's tech-driven business world.

Entry criteria

  • Applicants should typically hold an undergraduate degree with at least a 2:2 or an equivalent international qualification.
  • Professional qualifications equivalent to the required academic standards will be considered on a case-by-case basis.
  • Work experience is not required, but candidates without formal qualifications who have significant relevant professional experience will be considered individually.
