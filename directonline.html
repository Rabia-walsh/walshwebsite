UE Campus

UE Campus has partnered with Walsh College to offer students a flexible and comprehensive learning experience. Through this collaboration, students can pursue their Walsh College degree via UE Campus, benefiting from online classes and studies supported by UE's robust platform. This partnership allows students to choose from three study modes, with the convenience and accessibility of online education being fully integrated through UE Campus, ensuring a seamless path to achieving their academic goals.
