Walsh College, UAE

Walsh College, a distinguished American institution now in the UAE, brings its globally recognized business and technology education to the region. Accredited by prestigious bodies in the USA, Walsh ensures the highest standards of academic excellence. Students in the UAE benefit from the same rigorous curriculum and have the unique option to transfer seamlessly between the UAE and USA campuses, enhancing their educational experience. This connectivity between the two locations also opens doors to extensive career opportunities, bridging the gap between regional and international markets, and preparing graduates for success on a global scale.

Our History!

-----------------------------------

Established in 1922 by Mervyn B. Walsh, Thomas Edison’s accountant, Walsh College has been a leader in academic excellence for over 100 years. Originally founded as Walsh Institute to teach accounting, the college expanded rapidly, especially after WWII.

-----------------------------------

In 1968, it became Walsh College of Accountancy and Business Administration, offering its first bachelor’s degrees in 1970 and introducing a Master of Science in Taxation in 1974.

-----------------------------------

For 102 years, Walsh College has remained committed to delivering innovative education in business and technology.

Accreditation

National Secuirity Agency - NSA

Walsh College is recognized as a Center of Academic Excellence in Cyber Defense Cybersecurity Education by the NSA (National Security Agency Center of Academic Excellence). Walsh College aligns cybersecurity coursework with NSA defined standards.

Accreditation Council for Business Schools and Programs - ACPSP

Walsh is accredited Council for Business Schools and Programs (ACBSP) which is the leading specialized accreditation association for business education supporting, celebrating, and rewarding teaching excellence.

The Higher Learning Commission - HLC

Walsh is accredited by The Higher Learning Commission , a regional accreditation agency recognized by the U.S. Department of Education.

World Education Services - WES

Walsh College is WES-approved, ensuring global recognition of its degrees, which supports further education and career opportunities worldwide
