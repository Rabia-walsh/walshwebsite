Program Overview

The Master of Business Administration (MBA) at Walsh is an internationally ranked program designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of business essentials like accounting, finance, IT, business law, communication, and analytics. For those who want to go beyond, Walsh offers an exciting dual degree option. By completing just a few additional courses, you can earn both an MBA and a Master of Science in fields such as Accountancy, Finance, IT Leadership, Management, or Marketing. Elevate your career prospects and become a leader in today's dynamic business world!

Program Details

  • Award Duration: 2 Years
  • Mode Delivery: Hybrid | Oncampus
  • Annual Fees: 45,500+5% VAT
  • Intake: September, January & May

Program Outcomes

At Walsh, upon completing the Master of Science in Finance program, students will be equipped to strategize business expansion, manage risks and assets, optimize financial performance, and construct investment portfolios. The curriculum aligns with the Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) certification, covering essential topics like equity investments, fixed income securities, and financial analysis. Additionally, the program provides foundational knowledge relevant to the Certified Financial Planner (CFP) certification, focusing on personal financial planning, wealth management, and tax planning. This prepares graduates for success in both corporate finance and individual financial advising roles.

Entry criteria

  • Applicants should typically hold an undergraduate degree with at least a 2:2 or an equivalent international qualification.
  • Professional qualifications equivalent to the required academic standards will be considered on a case-by-case basis.
  • Work experience is not required, but candidates without formal qualifications who have significant relevant professional experience will be considered individually.
