Program Overview

The PhD in Technology – Data Analytics Specialization at Walsh College is designed for professionals who aspire to lead with data-driven precision in an increasingly complex technological landscape. As organizations grapple with vast amounts of data, from internal metrics to global economic trends, the ability to extract actionable insights is more critical than ever. This doctoral program equips you with the advanced tools, research skills, and analytical expertise needed to transform data into strategic decisions that drive success. The curriculum covers a comprehensive range of topics, from advanced data analytics and predictive modeling to the latest research in data-driven decision-making. Upon completion, you will be prepared to lead organizations with confidence, leveraging data to craft strategies that are both innovative and effective.

Program Outcomes

Graduates of the PhD in Technology – Data Analytics Specialization at Walsh College will emerge as leaders in data-driven decision-making, equipped with the ability to apply advanced analytics techniques to real-world challenges. They will have mastered predictive modeling, enabling them to forecast trends and identify opportunities within vast datasets. Additionally, they will be proficient in conducting high-impact research, contributing valuable insights to the field of data analytics. Graduates will possess the skills to integrate data analytics with broader technological and business strategies, addressing complex problems with innovative solutions. Furthermore, they will be adept at navigating the challenges of Big Data, including data governance, privacy, and security. Ultimately, graduates will be prepared to lead initiatives that leverage data to drive innovation and position their organizations at the forefront of their industries.

THE PHD TECHNOLOGY IN DATA ANALYTICS PREPARES YOU FOR THE CAREERS IN:

  • Data Scientist
  • Chief Data Officer
  • Technology Consultant
  • Research Scientist in Data Analytics
  • Director of Business Intelligence
  • Academic Faculty in Data Analytics
  • Big Data Analyst
  • Analytics Strategy Leader

Accreditations

The Higher Learning Commission (HLC)

Walsh is accredited by The Higher Learning Commission (hlcommission.org), a regional accreditation agency recognized by the U.S. Department of Education.

Accreditation Council for Business Schools and Programs (ACBSP)

Walsh College holds prestigious accreditations that signify its commitment to high-quality business education. ACBSP is renowned for its focus on teaching excellence and is a leading specialized accreditation body for business education.

Entry criteria

  • Applicants should typically hold an undergraduate degree with at least a 2:2 or an equivalent international qualification.
  • Professional qualifications equivalent to the required academic standards will be considered on a case-by-case basis.
  • Work experience is not required, but candidates without formal qualifications who have significant relevant professional experience will be considered individually.
