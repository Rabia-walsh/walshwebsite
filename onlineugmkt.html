Program Overview

Unlock your potential with Walsh College's BBA in Marketing. This dynamic program merges essential marketing concepts with a robust business foundation, including courses in accounting, advertising, communications, economics, finance, statistics, and technology. Dive into hands-on learning with practical exercises designed to sharpen your skills and elevate your knowledge. Our industry-savvy faculty, boasting extensive experience and advanced qualifications, will guide you through a versatile curriculum tailored for career success in marketing, advertising, and public relations. Transform your passion for marketing into a thriving career with Walsh College!

Program Outcomes

Walsh College's marketing program delivers a strong foundation in key marketing principles, from market research and consumer behavior to strategic planning. Students master skills in developing and executing marketing plans, including market segmentation and target audience identification. The program highlights digital marketing, covering social media, content marketing, and online advertising, preparing graduates to excel in the digital space. Students also gain expertise in market research and analysis for informed decision-making. Courses in brand management, advertising, and promotion teach students to build strong brands and create effective marketing strategies. The curriculum explores consumer psychology to design targeted campaigns and fosters creativity and innovation to adapt to market trends. Strong communication skills are emphasized, enabling graduates to convey marketing messages effectively. Networking opportunities through internships and industry events support career advancement. The program also offers a global marketing perspective and develops entrepreneurial skills, with potential alignment to industry certifications like those from the AMA or CIM.

THE BBA IN MARKETING COMBINES ACADEMIC THEORY AND HANDS-ON LEARNING EXPERIENCES TO PREPARE YOU FOR A CAREER IN:

  • Marketing Manager
  • Digital Marketing Specialist
  • Marketing Research Analyst
  • Social Media Manager
