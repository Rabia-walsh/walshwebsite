Walsh USA

Walsh College is a business and technology university offering undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral programs. It specializes in preparing students for careers in business and technology with a focus on practical, industry-relevant education. The college is dedicated to equipping students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in the rapidly evolving professional landscape.

Student Life

Walsh College offers a vibrant and engaging student life experience, with a range of services and activities designed to enhance campus life. From competitive sports and fitness programs to diverse student organizations and events, Walsh College provides ample opportunities for students to connect, stay active, and develop new skills outside the classroom. The supportive community and various extracurricular options contribute to a well-rounded and fulfilling college experience.

Path to Success

Walsh College’s path to success combines rigorous academics with hands-on experience and personalized support. With strong industry connections and dedicated faculty, students are well-prepared for their careers and equipped to achieve their professional goals.

Accreditation Matters

Walsh is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission (HLC) www.hlcommission.org, and has received specialized accreditation for select business degree programs by the Accreditation Council for Business Schools and Programs (ACBSP) www.acbsp.org.

ACBSP accreditation focuses on the quality of instruction and the development of practical skills that employers want. Walsh’s accreditation aligns with our mission to integrate theory and application to prepare graduates for successful careers. Visit the accreditation web page for a list of programs that are accredited by the ACBSP.

In 2020, Walsh earned a full 10-year reaffirmation of accreditation from The Higher Learning Commission (HLC) with no requirement for interim monitoring or reporting.

Approved

Walsh has been granted the legal authority to operate as a private, not-for-profit institution by the State of Michigan, Department of Labor and Economic Opportunity to grant bachelor’s, master’s, and doctoral degrees and certificate programs.
