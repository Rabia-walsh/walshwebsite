Program Overview

Embark on a dynamic journey into the heart of technology with Walsh College's Bachelor of Science in Information Technology majoring in General Information Technology. This comprehensive program equips you with a diverse skill set to master the multifaceted realm of IT, from coding to cybersecurity. You'll develop versatile coding expertise across various programming languages, secure digital assets through advanced cybersecurity strategies, and manage IT infrastructure to meet organizational needs.

The program also focuses on innovation with emerging technologies, enabling you to drive IT solutions and analyze complex data to inform strategic decisions. You'll gain proficiency in cloud computing for scalability and learn to troubleshoot IT challenges with technical acumen. This curriculum is designed to prepare you for a broad range of IT roles, ensuring you remain a crucial player in shaping the digital future.

At Walsh College, all students are part of one of our two faculties. Each faculty offers a diverse array of courses, spearheads impactful research, and builds valuable industry connections that enrich our curriculum and align it with current market needs. The Faculty of IT and Technology encompasses Walsh’s innovative disciplines, offering a spectrum of courses from conventional to cutting-edge, with research recognized for its international excellence

Program Outcomes

Walsh College’s Bachelor of Science in Information Technology majoring in General Information Technology offers specialized expertise across key IT areas, from coding and cybersecurity to data analysis and cloud computing. The program emphasizes hands-on training with interactive coursework and labs, providing practical experience to enhance your problem-solving abilities and technical proficiency.

You’ll gain industry-relevant skills in managing and optimizing IT infrastructure, navigating emerging technologies, and troubleshooting complex IT challenges. Designed for career readiness, this curriculum prepares you for diverse roles in IT, offering networking opportunities through internships and industry connections. With a focus on critical thinking, strategic decision-making, and adaptability, Walsh College ensures you’re well-equipped to thrive in the evolving tech landscape and pursue advanced education if desired.

THE BSc GENERAL IT PROGRAM PREPARES STUDENTS FOR IN-DEMAND TECHNOLOGY CAREERS IN:

  • Software Developer
  • IT Consultant
  • Network Administrator
  • System Engineer
  • Cyber Security Specialist

Accreditations

The Higher Learning Commission (HLC)

Walsh is accredited by The Higher Learning Commission (hlcommission.org), a regional accreditation agency recognized by the U.S. Department of Education.

National Security Agency Center of Academic Excellence

Walsh College is recognized as a Center of Academic Excellence in Cyber Defense Cybersecurity Education by the NSA. Walsh College aligns cybersecurity coursework with NSA defined standards.
