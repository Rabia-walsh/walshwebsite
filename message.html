President's Message

As President of Walsh College, it is with great pride and excitement that we announce our strategic partnership with FEUC to extend our distinguished programs to the UAE. The launch of Walsh College UAE is not merely an expansion of our academic reach, it is a testament to our unwavering commitment to integrating global perspectives into our curriculum and cultivating an environment of innovation and collaborative learning. Together, we are dedicated to equipping our students with the advanced skills and knowledge required to navigate and lead in today’s complex global business and technology landscapes.

Walsh College UAE and Walsh College in the USA will engage in a close-knit collaboration to deliver a truly global and immersive educational experience. By harnessing the combined strengths of both campuses, we are positioned to offer an unparalleled learning journey that upholds the highest academic standards. This partnership will provide greater access for our students to leverage an extensive range of resources, while learning from distinguished faculty, and building valuable industry connections so they are well-versed in international business practices and at the forefront of emerging technologies.

At Walsh College, we take pride in our role as a leading institution in business and technology education, and we are committed to a holistic admissions process to open greater doors of access and opportunity to those who wish to earn a highly respected Walsh College degree. Our mission extends beyond academic excellence; we are resolutely focused on preparing our graduates to succeed in the global job market.

I wish to thank my colleague Dr. Dave Schippers, Walsh College’s Chief Academic Officer, for his vision in seeing this opportunity and for being instrumental in the collaboration for building this partnership. Our collective work symbolizes our deep commitment to creating a world-class educational platform that empowers future leaders to excel in an interconnected and rapidly evolving global economy. We eagerly anticipate the opportunities this collaboration will bring to our students, our institutions, and the broader community.
