Program Overview

The Walsh Master of Science in Management (MSM) is an intensive exploration of the essential components of organizational management. This program provides a comprehensive foundation in organizational behavior and operations, with opportunities to specialize in four distinct concentration areas. Tailored for students who may not yet have extensive work experience, the MSM equips you with leadership skills and management acumen, opening doors to diverse career paths and accelerating your professional growth from the moment you step into the field.
With 95% of Walsh faculty bringing real-world industry experience to the classroom, you'll benefit from a hands-on curriculum that teaches you how to apply your newfound knowledge and skills effectively in a business environment.

Program Details

  • Award Duration: 2 Years
  • Mode Delivery: Hybrid | Oncampus

Program Outcomes

Graduating with a Master of Science in Management from Walsh means you’ll be prepared to lead teams, manage organizations, and even drive entire industries forward. You’ll gain the tools and skills necessary to excel in human resources, organizational leadership, and a variety of other management roles. The program strengthens your ability to become a competent, well-rounded leader, equipped to meet the demands of any industry.

THE MASTERS OF SCIENCE IN MANAGMENT PREPARES YOU FOR THE CAREERS IN:

  • Project Manager
  • Social Media Manager
  • Human Resource Manager
  • Entrepreneur

Accreditations

The Higher Learning Commission (HLC)

Walsh is accredited by The Higher Learning Commission (hlcommission.org), a regional accreditation agency recognized by the U.S. Department of Education.

Accreditation Council for Business Schools and Programs (ACBSP)

Walsh College holds prestigious accreditations that signify its commitment to high-quality business education. ACBSP is renowned for its focus on teaching excellence and is a leading specialized accreditation body for business education.

Entry criteria

  • Applicants should typically hold an undergraduate degree with at least a 2:2 or an equivalent international qualification.
  • Professional qualifications equivalent to the required academic standards will be considered on a case-by-case basis.
  • Work experience is not required, but candidates without formal qualifications who have significant relevant professional experience will be considered individually.
