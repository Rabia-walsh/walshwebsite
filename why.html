More About Us

Walsh offers a unique combination of high-quality education and a supportive community. With a rich history of excellence and a commitment to student success, Walsh stands out as an institution that provides not only academic rigor but also a nurturing environment.

Our programs are designed to equip students with the skills needed for a successful career while also fostering personal growth. Whether you're interested in pursuing a degree in business, technology, or the arts, Walsh provides a comprehensive education that prepares you for the future.

Join us at Walsh, where education meets opportunity, and become part of a tradition of excellence that can help you achieve your dreams.

Accreditation

National Security Agency - NSA

Walsh College is recognized as a Center of Academic Excellence in Cyber Defense Cybersecurity Education by the NSA (National Security Agency Center of Academic Excellence). Walsh College aligns cybersecurity coursework with NSA defined standards.

Accreditation Council for Business Schools and Programs - ACPSP

Walsh is accredited by the Council for Business Schools and Programs (ACBSP), the leading specialized accreditation association for business education supporting, celebrating, and rewarding teaching excellence.

The Higher Learning Commission - HLC

Walsh is accredited by The Higher Learning Commission, a regional accreditation agency recognized by the U.S. Department of Education.

World Education Services - WES

Walsh College is WES-approved, ensuring global recognition of its degrees, which supports further education and career opportunities worldwide.
