Program Overview

The Walsh Master of Science in Marketing (MSM) degree program is tailored for individuals who aspire to become strategic leaders in the dynamic world of marketing. This program goes beyond the basics of marketing, offering an in-depth exploration of advanced strategies, data-driven decision-making, and innovative approaches that keep you ahead in an ever-evolving industry. Whether you're looking to enhance your current marketing expertise or pivot into a new marketing role, this program provides the tools and knowledge needed to excel.
With a curriculum crafted by seasoned marketing professionals, you'll gain a deep understanding of market research, consumer behavior, brand management, and digital marketing. The program is designed to empower you to navigate complex marketing landscapes, craft strategies that resonate with target audiences, and drive meaningful results for your organization.

Program Details

  • Award Duration: 2 Years
  • Mode Delivery: Hybrid | Oncampus

Program Outcomes

The program outcomes of the Walsh Master of Science in Marketing are designed to empower you with the ability to craft and execute effective marketing strategies, analyze consumer behavior, and leverage market research for data-driven decisions. You'll gain expertise in digital marketing, brand management, and global marketing trends, ensuring you're prepared to lead in a competitive marketplace. Graduates of this program will emerge as marketing innovators, equipped with the skills to drive brand growth, enhance customer engagement, and create impactful marketing campaigns that resonate in today’s dynamic business environment.

THE MASTERS OF SCIENCE IN MARKETING PREPARES YOU FOR THE CAREERS IN:

  • Marketing Manager
  • Brand Manager
  • Digital Marketing Strategist
  • Market Research Analyst
  • Social Media Manager
  • Product Manager
  • Advertising Director
  • Chief Marketing Officer (CMO)

Accreditations

The Higher Learning Commission (HLC)

Walsh is accredited by The Higher Learning Commission (hlcommission.org), a regional accreditation agency recognized by the U.S. Department of Education.

Accreditation Council for Business Schools and Programs (ACBSP)

Walsh College holds prestigious accreditations that signify its commitment to high-quality business education. ACBSP is renowned for its focus on teaching excellence and is a leading specialized accreditation body for business education.

Entry criteria

  • Applicants should typically hold an undergraduate degree with at least a 2:2 or an equivalent international qualification.
  • Professional qualifications equivalent to the required academic standards will be considered on a case-by-case basis.
  • Work experience is not required, but candidates without formal qualifications who have significant relevant professional experience will be considered individually.
