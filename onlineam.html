Program Overview

Embark on a transformative journey with the Bachelor of Science in Applied Management (BSAM) from Walsh College. This 18-month, fully online BSAM program is meticulously designed to provide you with a comprehensive understanding of applied management principles. Gain essential business skills and knowledge that will empower you to excel in various management environments and leadership roles. Walsh College's BSAM program is structured to support your career aspirations and provide a solid foundation in applied management. Each module enhances your understanding of key management concepts and develops practical skills that are immediately applicable in the professional world. With a focus on critical thinking, strategic planning, and leadership, this program prepares you for the complexities of the modern business landscape and sets you apart in the job market. This top-ranked online BSAM program offers flexible study schedules and personalized support, making it ideal for busy professionals and students with personal commitments. Our engaging learning experience includes occasional webinars led by industry experts, providing valuable insights and interactive learning opportunities. Additionally, we offer one-on-one sessions with tutors to provide personalized support and guidance whenever needed. UeCampus is committed to your success beyond the classroom. Our comprehensive support services include academic advising and mental health support, ensuring you have the resources you need to thrive.

Accreditations

Walsh College Accreditation: Walsh College is a highly respected institution in the United States, accredited by the Higher Learning Commission (HLC), one of the top accrediting bodies in the country. Walsh College also holds specialized accreditation for its business and accounting programs from the Accreditation Council for Business Schools and Programs (ACBSP). This accreditation ensures that our programs meet the highest standards of quality and excellence in education, providing you with a degree that is recognized and valued by employers worldwide. For our international students, this means that your degree from Walsh College carries significant prestige and will be respected across global job markets.

Qualifi Accreditation: Qualifi is a prestigious UK awarding body, regulated by Ofqual (The Office of Qualifications and Examinations Regulation), ensuring that all qualifications meet rigorous academic standards. This accreditation guarantees that the Level 4 and Level 5 Diplomas you earn are of high quality and are recognized globally. For international students, this means that your Qualifi diploma is a robust credential that can open doors to further education and career opportunities both in the UK and around the world.
