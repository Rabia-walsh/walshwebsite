Program Overview

Embark on a transformative journey with Walsh College’s Bachelor of Science in Information Technology major in Data Analytics. This program equips you with essential skills in data collection, analysis, and visualization, empowering you to extract actionable insights from complex datasets. Through a dynamic curriculum, you’ll master key areas such as Project Management, Machine Learning, Data Visualization, Predictive Modeling, and Data Storage Technologies. You’ll also engage in hands-on learning with courses like Business Analytics, Problem Solving, Principles of Data Analytics, and Data-Driven Decision Making, preparing you to tackle real-world challenges and drive innovation in any organization.

At Walsh College, you’ll gain practical experience and advanced knowledge through interactive coursework and projects designed to simulate industry scenarios. This program’s focus on cutting-edge technologies and methodologies ensures you are well-prepared to become a leading data analyst. You’ll learn to leverage data to enhance decision-making processes, optimize business strategies, and contribute to organizational success. With a strong foundation in both theoretical and practical aspects of data analytics, you will be ready to make a significant impact in the rapidly evolving field of data science.

Program Outcomes

Walsh College’s Bachelor of Science in Information Technology major in Data Analytics offers specialized expertise in data collection, analysis, and visualization. The program emphasizes hands-on training through interactive coursework and real-world scenarios, equipping you with industry-relevant skills in machine learning, predictive modeling, and business analytics.

You'll develop critical thinking and problem-solving abilities essential for turning complex data into actionable insights. Networking opportunities with industry professionals, combined with a curriculum designed for career readiness and leadership development, prepare you for various roles in the rapidly growing field of data analytics. This program also provides a solid foundation for advanced education and continuous professional growth.

THE BSc GENERAL IT PROGRAM PREPARES STUDENTS FOR IN-DEMAND TECHNOLOGY CAREERS IN:

  • Data Analyst
  • Business Intelligence (BI) Analyst
  • Data Scientist
  • Data Engineer
  • Machine Learning Engineer
  • Business Analytics Consultant

Accreditations

The Higher Learning Commission (HLC)

Walsh is accredited by The Higher Learning Commission (hlcommission.org), a regional accreditation agency recognized by the U.S. Department of Education.

National Security Agency Center of Academic Excellence

Walsh College is recognized as a Center of Academic Excellence in Cyber Defense Cybersecurity Education by the NSA. Walsh College aligns cybersecurity coursework with NSA defined standards.
